Explanation

The overall humidity does not increase past 100 percent. The lowest humidity level is at 0 percent between a 60 and 80 latitude level. The humidity level cannot go over 100 percent because air cannot be saturated with anymore water vapor. When the humidity level is above 100 percent, that is known as super-saturation, and is not possible in nature. The humidity level range from -60 on the altitude scale to 80 on the altitude scale.
