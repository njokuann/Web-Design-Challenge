Explanation

As the latitude for the cities increased, the maximum temperature gradually decreased. When the latitude increases, the temperature gets colder in areas of high altitudes, usually mountains. When the latitude decreases and gets closer and above 0, the temperature increases such as in Africa. Areas that are near the equator have high max temperatures.
