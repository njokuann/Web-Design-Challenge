Explanation

The cloudiness levels are static and uniform. The cloudiness levels range from 0 to 100 percent. The latitude levels range from -60 to 80. Between 0 and about 75 percent of the cloudiness levels the cities are more varied. At 0 the cities are more bunched at a 0 percent cloudiness levels. Areas that have limited sunshine are more prone to have higher cloudiness levels, especially areas that are located in subtropical levels which lead to high humidity.
